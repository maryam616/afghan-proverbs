"صبر تلخ است ولی میوه‌اش شیرین است"

"Patience is bitter, but its fruit is sweet."

This means patience which will lead us to rewards.

"هرکه بامش بیش، برفش بیشتر"

"The taller the roof, the more snow it catches."

With power comes more responsibility.

"چاه مکن بهر کسی اول خودت، دوم کسی"

"Don’t dig a hole for others because you might fall in it yourself."

Don’t plan harm for others.

"پای مرغ به نمکدان برسد، کارش تمام است"

"Once the chicken steps into the salt bowl, it will be done for it."

Some mistakes are final stage and you maybe done.
